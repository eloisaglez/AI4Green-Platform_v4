## About AI4Green

AI4Green is an Erasmus+ funded educational partnership focused on integrating artificial intelligence and sustainability in STEAM education across Europe.

### Project Goals

1. **Develop innovative educational resources** that combine AI and environmental sustainability
2. **Train educators** in AI technologies and green teaching methodologies
3. **Foster international collaboration** among schools and educational institutions
4. **Promote student engagement** in environmental issues through technology

### Project Timeline

**Duration**: 2024-2027

**Phase 1** (2024): Curriculum development and resource creation  
**Phase 2** (2025): Pilot testing in partner schools  
**Phase 3** (2026-2027): Implementation and evaluation

### Key Activities

#### Teacher Training Workshops
Professional development sessions on AI tools and sustainable education practices.

#### Student Competitions
Annual AI4Green challenges where students solve real environmental problems.

#### Resource Development
Creating open educational resources for the global teaching community.

#### Research & Evaluation
Measuring the impact of AI-enhanced environmental education.

## Project Outcomes

### Expected Results

- 50+ educational modules on AI and sustainability
- 200+ teachers trained across partner countries
- 5,000+ students reached
- Open-access digital platform (this site!)
- Best practices guide for educators

### Sustainability Impact

Our project aims to raise environmental awareness and empower the next generation to use technology for positive change.
